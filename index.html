About Us

The Dana-Farber Cancer Institute is a world leader in adult and pediatric cancer treatment and research. Our mission is to provide expert, compassionate care to children and adults and to advance the understanding, diagnosis, treatment, cure, and prevention of cancer and related diseases.

Cancer Treatments

Breast Cancer

We offer comprehensive breast cancer care, from diagnosis to advanced treatments. Our multidisciplinary team is dedicated to providing personalized care for each patient.

Lung Cancer

Our lung cancer program provides state-of-the-art treatment options including surgery, radiation therapy, and targeted therapies, all tailored to meet the unique needs of our patients.

Leukemia

We specialize in the treatment of leukemia with innovative therapies and a team of experts who are at the forefront of leukemia research and treatment.

Colorectal Cancer

Our colorectal cancer team offers a comprehensive approach to treatment, including surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation, supported by cutting-edge research.

Prostate Cancer

Our prostate cancer specialists provide advanced diagnostic and treatment options, including robotic surgery and precision medicine, to ensure the best possible outcomes.

Skin Cancer

We offer the latest in skin cancer treatments, including Mohs surgery, immunotherapy, and targeted therapies, delivered by a team of dedicated specialists.

Research

At Dana-Farber, our researchers are working tirelessly to find new treatments for cancer. We are at the forefront of groundbreaking research that is making a difference in the lives of patients around the world.
